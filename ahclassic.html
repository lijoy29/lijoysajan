Simulating a Consulting Firm For

AH Classic

Module: BNM803 Developing Business System Workshop

As a Master's student in Business Analysis and Information Systems at Aston University, I had the privilege of undertaking the Developing Business Systems Workshop module, guided by the exceptional mentorship of Prof. Victoria Uren and Prof. Priscilla Omonedo.

This module was transformative, offering not only technical and career-specific insights but also invaluable lessons in empathy and cultural diversity. Through a dynamic group project simulating a consulting firm scenario, we engaged with a case study where we acted as consultants for a client. The experience involved conducting client interviews to understand their needs and challenges, defining budgets, researching root causes, and delivering tailored solutions. We developed professional business proposals and presentations while honing teamwork and task management skills, learning from each other throughout the process.

With consistent feedback from our professors, I gained a deeper understanding of professional standards, problem-solving strategies, and effective communication. This hands-on experience felt like real-world consulting, preparing me to approach business challenges with confidence and precision. I am deeply grateful to my professors for their guidance and support, which has been instrumental in my growth.
